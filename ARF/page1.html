Wanna go to Sri Lanka? Better not have a Buddha Tattoo

If you have a Buddha tattoo on your body you better not visit Sri Lanka or keep it covered all the time. Actually, almost 70% of people in Sri Lanka follows Buddhism and most people are intolerant for any kind of insult of Buddha. Having a Buddha Tattoo is considered as an insult and for this you maybe even sent back to your country from airport. Although there is no rule for banning Buddha Tattoo in their Constitution, but still better be on a safer side.

Doctor's Sloppy Writing can be the reason for Death.

Never thought of handwriting being the reason of death right but here is the reality check. How many of you can understand a prescription written by a doctor?? I think very few… and just like us many other doctors or chemists may not understand the prescription, misunderstands the words and can give a different medicine. According to a study, just in US, 7000 people die just because of getting wrong medicines due to the sloppy handwriting of the Doctor. So, just imagine what would be the number in the world ? Just a tip to doctors out there, "Please improve your handwriting you guys".

Why Does a Jellyfish Evaporate Under the Sun?

You can face a situation where you see a jellyfish on the land and if it's unattended then after sometimes… Boom !!!! Most of it is gone. Jellyfish are mainly made up of water. You can find a jellyfish that is 98% water or at least it will be made up of 95% of water. Now if it is on land, under sun, it will start getting evaporated (it is tendency of water to get evaporated because of heat).

Water makes different sounds as per it's temperature.

The hot water and cold water, both produces a different sound when poured. Ever noticed that? I think you haven't. But here I will tell you the reason and you can experiment it at home. Basically, the heat changes the thickness or viscosity of the water and that results in the pitch of the sound that water produces. Actually the heat comes from the molecules of water moving. In hot water molecules moves faster, the thickness is less and makes slightly low-pitched sound whereas cold water is thicker a makes a bit high-pitched sound.

This brief, ensuring laugh helps in social bonding too.
